About Us

Wesco Exteriors has over 20 years of experience in helping homeowners create the home of their dreams. We specialize in siding, shakes, board and batten, rain gutter, downspouts, soffit, porches, and all other kinds of exterior work!

Whether you're in the process of building your home from the ground up, or looking to update your current home, we have experience from A to Z.

No job is too big or too small for us. From a new shed in the backyard, to a multiunit apartment— we do work from Cedar City in southern Utah to Logan in the north to bring homeowners quality work they can count on.
